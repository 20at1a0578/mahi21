Understanding the Philosophy of Ramanuja

Published on March 27, 2025 | By Sanatan Learning

Ramanuja was the founder of Vishishtadvaita Vedanta, which teaches that Brahman has both unity and diversity.

Key Teachings

  • Vishishtadvaita: The soul and Brahman are distinct, but the soul depends on Brahman.
  • Devotion (Bhakti): The path to liberation is through devotion to Vishnu.
