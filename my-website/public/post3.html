The Teachings of Adi Shankaracharya

Published on March 27, 2025 | By Sanatan Learning

Adi Shankaracharya was a great philosopher and reformer who established the doctrine of Advaita Vedanta.

Key Concepts

  • Advaita Vedanta: The belief that everything is one and Brahman is the only reality.
  • Maya: The world is an illusion, and only the soul is eternal.
